Overview

Purpose

Sunlit Homes aims to provide information about skylights, their benefits, installation processes, and how they enhance natural light in homes. Our goal is to help homeowners make informed decisions about incorporating skylights into their living spaces, maximizing sunlight, and improving energy efficiency.

Audience

Our website caters to homeowners, architects, and interior designers interested in incorporating skylights into their buildings. Whether you're looking to brighten up a room, improve energy efficiency, or enhance the aesthetic of your home, we provide the resources and guidance you need to make the best choice for your space.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: Ana, the Conscious Homeowner

Ana is a homeowner living in a region with harsh winters and short daylight hours. She is looking for ways to reduce her energy costs while also bringing more natural light into her living room, where she spends most of her time with family. Ana visits the Sunlit Homes website to learn how skylights can help save on heating costs and create a cozier atmosphere. She uses the "Benefits" section to understand the advantages and finds inspiration in the photos to choose the right style.

Scenario 2: Robert, the Innovative Architect

Robert is a freelance architect designing a modern home for a demanding client. He wants to ensure the project is environmentally sustainable and maximizes natural light. Robert explores the Sunlit Homes website to find creative design ideas and learn about different types of skylights. He uses the site as a reference to suggest innovative options and shares the link with his client to help them visualize the impact on the design.
